# Data Analyst Boolean Search

## Core Search
```text
("Data Analyst" OR "Business Analyst")
AND
(SQL OR Excel OR Power BI OR Tableau)
NOT
(Intern OR Trainee)
```

## Advanced Search
```text
("Data Analyst")
AND
(SQL AND (Excel OR Power BI OR Tableau))
```

## Purpose
Find professionals working in reporting, dashboards, and business insights.

## Best For
- Reporting Roles
- BI Teams
- Product Analytics
- Business Insights

## Recruiter Tips
- SQL is mandatory
- Power BI is very high demand
- Excel is baseline skill for entry roles

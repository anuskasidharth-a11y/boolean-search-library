# AI Python Developer Boolean Search

## Search Strings

### Core Search
```text
("AI Engineer" OR "Machine Learning Engineer" OR "Python Developer")
AND
(Python AND (Machine Learning OR AI OR NLP))
NOT
(Intern OR Trainee)
```

---

### Advanced Search
```text
("Data Scientist" OR "AI Engineer")
AND
(Python AND (TensorFlow OR PyTorch OR Scikit-learn))
AND
(Deep Learning OR NLP)
```

---

## Purpose

Find Python developers working in AI, machine learning, and data science roles.

---

## Best For

- AI/ML Engineering Roles
- Data Science Teams
- Product Based AI Teams
- Research & Development Roles

---

## Recruiter Tips

- Always include TensorFlow and PyTorch for AI roles.
- Add NLP for language-based AI hiring.
- Include Data Scientist as alternate title.

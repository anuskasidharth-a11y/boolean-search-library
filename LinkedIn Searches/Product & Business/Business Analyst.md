# Product & Business Boolean Search

## Business Analyst
```text
("Business Analyst")
AND
(SQL OR Agile OR Scrum OR Jira)
NOT
(Intern)
```

## Product Manager
```text
("Product Manager")
AND
(Agile OR Roadmap OR UX OR Strategy)
```

## Scrum Master
```text
("Scrum Master")
AND
(Agile OR Jira OR Sprint)
```

## Purpose
Find product and business professionals in Agile environments.

## Recruiter Tips
- Jira is essential for Agile roles
- Product roles require roadmap + strategy keywords

# Talent Acquisition Boolean Search

## IT Recruiter Search
```text
("Technical Recruiter" OR "Talent Acquisition Specialist")
AND
(IT OR Technical OR Engineering)
NOT
(Intern)
```

## General Recruiter Search
```text
(Recruiter OR "HR Recruiter")
AND
(Sourcing OR Hiring)
```

## Purpose
Find recruiters handling IT and non-IT hiring.

## Recruiter Tips
- Always include Technical Recruiter keyword
- Add LinkedIn Recruiter if needed

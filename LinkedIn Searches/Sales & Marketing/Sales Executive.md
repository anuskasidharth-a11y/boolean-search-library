# Sales Executive Boolean Search

## Sales Roles
```text
("Sales Executive" OR "Business Development Executive" OR "Account Manager")
AND
(Sales OR B2B OR CRM)
NOT
(Intern)
```

## Marketing Roles
```text
("Digital Marketing Specialist" OR "SEO Specialist" OR "Content Marketer")
AND
(Digital OR SEO OR Social Media)
```

## Purpose
Find sales and marketing professionals in B2B and digital roles.

## Recruiter Tips
- Separate sales vs marketing clearly
- SaaS roles require CRM experience

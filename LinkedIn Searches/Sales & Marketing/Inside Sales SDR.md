# Inside Sales / SDR Boolean Search

## Core Search
```text
("Inside Sales" OR "Sales Development Representative" OR SDR)
AND
(Lead Generation OR Cold Calling OR Outreach OR Prospecting)
NOT
(Intern)
```

## Purpose
Find top-of-funnel sales professionals responsible for lead generation.

## Best For
- SaaS Companies
- B2B Sales Teams
- Startup Sales Hiring

## Recruiter Tips
- SDR is critical for SaaS hiring
- Always include "Lead Generation"
- Filter by communication-heavy roles

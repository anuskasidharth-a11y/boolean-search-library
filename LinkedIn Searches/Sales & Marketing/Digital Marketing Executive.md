# Digital Marketing Boolean Search

## Core Search
```text
("Digital Marketing Executive" OR "Marketing Executive")
AND
(SEO OR SEM OR Social Media OR Content Marketing)
NOT
(Intern)
```

## Performance Marketing (High Demand)
```text
("Performance Marketing Specialist")
AND
(Google Ads OR Meta Ads OR PPC OR Paid Ads)
```

## Purpose
Find marketing professionals in digital growth and brand marketing.

## Best For
- Startup Marketing Teams
- Growth Marketing Roles
- E-commerce Companies

## Recruiter Tips
- Performance marketing is high-paying skill
- SEO + Content = organic marketing roles
- Paid ads = fast-growing demand skill
